REVIEWS

Luffy

Wow, just had the most amazing milk tea experience! The blend of black tea and creamy milk was perfection. It had just the right balance of sweetness, and the boba pearls were chewy and delightful. This milk tea spot has definitely earned a spot in my regular rotation. Highly recommend for anyone craving a comforting and flavorful beverage!

Robin Chwan

I tried a new milk tea place today, and I must say, their signature milk tea was quite unique. The tea flavor was strong and robust, and the addition of condensed milk gave it a rich and indulgent taste. The only downside was that it was a bit too sweet for my liking. Nevertheless, the cozy ambiance of the cafe and the friendly staff made it an overall enjoyable experience. Will be back to explore more options on their menu!

Marimo

Decided to give this trendy milk tea joint a shot, and while the atmosphere was lively, I wasn't blown away by the milk tea. It lacked the depth of flavor I was expecting, and the pearls were a bit too soft for my taste. On the plus side, the presentation was Instagram-worthy, and the service was quick. Maybe I'll try a different flavor next time and see if it changes my opinion.

Kawaii Choppah

I'm officially obsessed with the rose milk tea from this little tea shop! The floral notes combined with the creamy milk create a delightful and sophisticated flavor. The boba pearls were perfectly cooked, adding a fun texture to each sip. It's a bit on the pricier side, but the quality is worth it. This might just be the best milk tea I've ever had!
